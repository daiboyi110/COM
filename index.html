About This Application

üìã Features

  • Video & Image Analysis: Process both video files and static images
  • MediaPipe Pose Estimation: Automatic detection of 33 body landmarks
  • 2D & 3D Analysis Modes: Choose between 2D pixel-based or 3D world coordinate analysis
  • Center of Mass Calculation: Calculate segment COM and total-body COM with sex-specific models (de Leva, 1996)
  • Bilateral Symmetry Mirroring: Automatically estimate missing landmarks using opposite side data
  • Interactive Editing: Manually adjust joint positions and calibration points
  • Coordinate System Visualization: Display 2D or 3D coordinate axes with origin markers
  • Data Export: Export pose data to Excel, export images/frames with overlays, and export videos with overlays

üî¨ Technical Details

  • Pose Estimation: MediaPipe Pose v0.5+ (Google)
  • Landmarks Tracked: 50 total points (33 base + 17 calculated)
  • Landmarks Displayed: 37 points (20 base + 17 calculated)
  • Visibility Threshold: 0.3 (landmarks with visibility < 0.3 are hidden)
  • 2D Coordinate System: Origin at left-bottom corner, calibrated to meters
  • 3D Coordinate System: Origin at mid-hip, coordinates in meters
  • Processing: Client-side only (no data uploaded to servers)
  • Browser Compatibility: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+

üìä Sex-Specific Models

The application uses different segment mass percentages and center of mass locations for males and females based on biomechanical research by de Leva (1996):

Male Model

  • Head+Neck: 6.94% of body mass, COM at 50.00%
  • Trunk: 43.46%, COM at 43.10%
  • Upper Arm: 2.71% each, COM at 57.72%
  • Forearm: 1.62% each, COM at 45.74%
  • Hand: 0.61% each, COM at 79.00%
  • Thigh: 14.16% each, COM at 40.95%
  • Shank: 4.33% each, COM at 44.59%
  • Foot: 1.37% each, COM at 44.15%

Female Model

  • Head+Neck: 6.68% of body mass, COM at 50.00%
  • Trunk: 42.57%, COM at 37.82%
  • Upper Arm: 2.55% each, COM at 57.54%
  • Forearm: 1.38% each, COM at 45.59%
  • Hand: 0.56% each, COM at 74.74%
  • Thigh: 14.78% each, COM at 36.12%
  • Shank: 4.81% each, COM at 44.16%
  • Foot: 1.29% each, COM at 40.14%

Note: COM percentages indicate location from proximal to distal joint. Total-body COM is calculated as weighted average of all 12 segment COMs.
